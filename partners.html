Volley Talk with Sarah Pavan

Hosted by Olympian, World Champion, and former Nebraska Cornhusker All-American Sarah Pavan and her husband Adam Schulz—international volleyball coach and stats wizard—Volley Talk is the podcast for fans who want to dig deep into NCAA and international volleyball. With weekly highlights, feature match breakdowns, and hot takes drawn from decades of experience at the highest level, they bring unmatched insight to volleyball fans everywhere. Join their official fantasy league on VOLI and compete alongside their community.

Major League Minute

Your quick-hitting podcast diving into the fast-paced world of Major League Volleyball. Join Nick Prososki (Doc) and Gina Mancuso-Prososki (GMP)—former Nebraska Cornhusker and Omaha Supernovas player—as they break down the biggest games, key performances, and trending headlines with hot takes and real talk. We also partner with MLM for exclusive player Ask Me Anythings on VOLI. Join their official fantasy league and compete alongside their community. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just keeping up with the season, Major League Minute keeps you dialed in and ready for the next serve.

Court Captains

Former college volleyball players Kelly and Nikki team up with their volley newbie friend Ian to bring you all the tea on Major League Volleyball. This up-and-coming fan podcast has built a loyal following with their unfiltered hot takes, primarily covering MLV with the occasional dive into "LOVBuh". If you want the real talk, the drama, and hosts who aren't afraid to speak their minds, Court Captains has you covered.
